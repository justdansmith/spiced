# The SPICED namespaces

SPICED is not a sales methodology here. It is the protocol every GTM skill plugs into. Each element is a namespace: a fixed, shared place where skills write typed values and any agent can read them. The field names are the contract.

| Letter | Namespace | What it holds |
|--------|-----------|---------------|
| S | Situation | Account, persona, and context: who they are, where they are, what is true today. |
| P | Pain | Problems, risks, and the why behind resistance. |
| I | Impact | Rational and emotional recurring value, the two forces of recurring revenue. |
| CE | Critical Event | Impact under a time constraint: urgency, and the pull that engages the Decider. |
| D | Decision | Process, criteria, makers, and the buying group. Multi-threading and stakeholder work nest here. |

A sixth group, X (Cross-Cutting), holds Bowtie, Revenue Architecture, Growth Architecture, governance, manager, and facilitation skills that span the lifecycle or sit above a single letter. These are the long-term moat.

A deal is fully diagnosed when each of S, P, I, CE, and D has at least one skill writing into it. That composite is the SPICED-completeness scorecard.
